CESAR VALENTINO'S TIMELINE

Cesar is Born! 1968

Born in Passaic, New Jersey.

Year - 1974

Family moves to Washington Heights. Starts roller skating, figure skating, color guard, and twriling of batons and props.

button

Year - 1981

Attended and excelled in the arts program at PS 189. Did vocal, guitar, drama and art

button

Year - 1982

In junior high school ( JHS 143), Cesar style changed to punk rock and started going to uptown rave parties and riverside jams.

button

1983 - Vogue

Cesar goes to the West Side Christopher Street Piers for the first time and discovers vogue dance, immediately starts learning the craft.

button

1984 - Meeting Mentors

Cesar gets introduced to early vogue mentors: Willie Ninja and Little Michael Xtravaganza.

button

Year - 1985

House Music enters the dancing scene, inspries Cesar to create his own signature sytle of Vogue.

button

Recruited - 1986

Recruited by Willie Ninja to be a member of the House of Ninja.

button

Year - 1987 - 1988

The early club days begin at nightclubs such as 'Better Days' and 'Tracks'. Cesar becomes popular for his signature style of voguing, popping, dipping, and spinning with grace and precision. He walks his first ball, the 'Paris is Buring' Ball and wins for 'Butch Queen Realness' category. Recrutied as the Father of the House of Magnifique, with the blessing of Willi Ninja. He was featured in 'The Village Voice', in the first ever article about the Harlem Drag Ballroom Scene, and is aprinciple dancer on 'The Latin Connection' on television.

button

1989

Ceasr competes in 'The Love Ball', a AIDS fundraising event run by Susanne Bartsch. Tours the world with Malcolm Mclauren. Featured in 'Pazzo Magazine' and 'Nightlife Magazine'. Makes apperances in 'Paris is Burning' and 'The House of Tres' documentaries. He is also the first time choreographer for contestants competing in Miss Continental USA.

button

Year 1990

Cesar stays active in the Ballroom scene, competing in catergories such as'New Wave vs Punk Rock', "Futuristic Bizzare", 'Hair Couture', 'Butch Queen Realness', 'Designer's Delight', and 'Butch Queen Runway'.

1991

Cesar is presented with the Marcel Christian Legend Award.

button

1993

Walked and won many balls. Began to model and dance professionally. Decided to focus on a career in behavorial science research to work with the high risk drug using populations and left the ballroom scene.

button

1994

Cesar is principle musician, actor, and dancer in musical videos with Carmen Electra, K7, and Toni Braxton.

button

1995

Cesar joins agencies like Noir and Mega Management and enrolls in professional dance classes.

button

1995-2006

Modeled and dance professionally.

button

1995-2013

Worked as a choreographer, dancer, and clothing designer for beauty pageant contestants and female impersonators.

button

2010 - 2011

Cesar teaches first Vogue class at Peridance and begins working in the wardrobe department at Alvin Ailey.

button

2011

Becomes faculty at Peridance.

button

2014

Recieves the 'Red Era of Ballroom Icon Award'. Teaches first offical Vogue class at Alvin Ailey and becomes an offical faculty member at the Ailey Extension. Travels the world and teaches workshops in Berlin, Sao Paulo, and the Dominican Republic.

button

2015

Recieved the 'Heritage Gatekeeper Award' and the 'House of Infinity Paved the Way Award' Cesar becomes posing and runway coach for the 'Simply B' UK campaign [link]. Cesar is principle Vogue Dancer for an Original Netflix Series called 'Get Down', directed by Baz Luhrmann.
